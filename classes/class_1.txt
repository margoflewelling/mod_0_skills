Employees

attributes:
name (string)
title (string)
year_started (integer)
can_work_nights? (boolean)
days_available (array)

methods:
promote_to_manager (changes title attribute to manager)
add_to_night_shift (changes can_work_nights to True)
years_employed (subtracts year_started from 2020)
available_friday (adds friday to the days_available array)
